# Easy Mode - Demo Script (60-90 seconds)

## Video Recording Checklist

Before recording:
- [ ] Fresh app install or cleared data
- [ ] Good lighting and stable device
- [ ] Notifications enabled
- [ ] Sound on for completion celebrations

---

## Demo Flow

### Scene 1: Welcome & Sign Up (10 seconds)
**Show:** Splash screen → Sign up screen

"Welcome to Easy Mode, your AI life coach for building confidence through Action, Audacity, and Enjoyment."

**Action:** Tap "Create Account" and quickly sign up with email.

---

### Scene 2: Onboarding (15 seconds)
**Show:** Onboarding quiz screens

"Let's personalize your experience. First, what's your biggest challenge?"

**Action:** 
1. Tap "I struggle to ask for what I want"
2. Continue → Select "Be more assertive and confident"  
3. Continue → Select "10 min" for daily time
4. Tap "Get Started"

---

### Scene 3: Home - Daily Task (20 seconds)
**Show:** Home screen with daily task card

"Here's your command center. Every day, you get one Easy Mode Moment - a simple task tailored to your goal."

**Action:**
1. Point out the XP bar and streak counter
2. Tap "Start Now" on the daily task
3. Show timer running briefly
4. Tap "I did it!" 
5. Show confetti celebration and XP award

"Just like that - 100 XP earned. Small wins build big confidence."

---

### Scene 4: Audacity Scripts (15 seconds)
**Show:** Scripts library → Script detail

"Ready to be bolder? Our Audacity Scripts give you word-for-word templates for asking for what you want."

**Action:**
1. Navigate to Audacity tab
2. Show script categories and risk levels
3. Tap a script (e.g., "Ask for a Small Discount")
4. Show the editable template

"You can practice, customize, and track your outcomes. Even a 'no' earns you XP - because trying is what matters."

---

### Scene 5: Joy Rituals (10 seconds)
**Show:** Rituals screen

"Don't forget to enjoy the journey. Pick a daily joy ritual to romanticize your everyday moments."

**Action:**
1. Show ritual grid
2. Select "Morning Playlist"
3. Show ritual steps

---

### Scene 6: Progress & Wrap-up (10 seconds)
**Show:** Progress screen

"Track your growth with XP, levels, and badges. Build streaks for bonus rewards."

**Action:** Show XP bar, stats, and badge collection

"Easy Mode - because confidence is built one small action at a time."

---

## Key Talking Points

1. **Three Principles**: Action, Audacity, Enjoyment
2. **XP System**: Gamified progress with streak bonuses
3. **Resilience Flow**: "I couldn't" shows supportive alternatives
4. **Personalization**: Onboarding tailors the experience
5. **Low Barrier**: 5-10 minute daily commitment
